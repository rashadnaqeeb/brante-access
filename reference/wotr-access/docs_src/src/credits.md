# Credits

Wrath Access is built and maintained by **bradjrenshaw**.

directly.

## Third-party components

The mod ships and builds on work by others:

- **Prism** — screen-reader speech.
- **NAudio** — audio playback.
- The **typeahead search** engine is ported from **OniAccess**, used with permission.

Patterns and lessons from the **Say the Spire** mods (1 and 2) informed much of the design.

Full third-party license texts are in
[`THIRD-PARTY-NOTICES.md`](https://github.com/bradjrenshaw/wotr-access/blob/main/THIRD-PARTY-NOTICES.md)
in the repository.
